FIVE TECH PREDICTIONS FOR 2023

1. Self-driving cars become mainstream and the majority of vehicles sold are fully autonomous.

Self-driving cars have been a topic of conversation for years, but in 2023, we may finally see them become mainstream. With advancements in technology and increased testing, the majority of vehicles sold could be fully autonomous. This has the potential to revolutionize transportation and reduce accidents caused by human error. Additionally, self-driving cars could have a significant impact on the economy, with the potential to create new industries and job opportunities.

2. Augmented Reality (AR) technology advances to the point where we see the widespread adoption of AR headsets, making virtual reality obsolete.

As AR technology advances, we may see a shift away from virtual reality (VR) towards augmented reality (AR). AR headsets could become more widespread, enabling us to overlay digital information onto the real world. This has the potential to revolutionize everything from gaming and entertainment to education and healthcare. With AR, we could create immersive experiences that blend the digital and physical worlds in new and exciting ways.

3. The use of blockchain technology becomes ubiquitous, with blockchain-based systems used for everything from supply chain management to voting.

Blockchain technology has the potential to revolutionize a wide range of industries, from finance to healthcare. By creating secure, decentralized systems, blockchain-based solutions could make everything from supply chain management to voting more transparent and efficient. With increased adoption, we could see a more secure and trusted digital landscape, with a greater emphasis on privacy and data protection.

4. Quantum computing becomes commercially viable, with the first quantum computers for consumers hitting the market.

Quantum computing has long been a topic of research, but we may finally see it become commercially viable in 2023. With the first quantum computers for consumers hitting the market, we could see a new era of computing power and speed. This has the potential to revolutionize everything from drug discovery and climate modeling to encryption and cybersecurity.

5. Artificial Intelligence (AI) reaches a new level of sophistication, enabling machines to perform complex tasks that were previously thought impossible for computers to achieve.

AI has been advancing rapidly in recent years, but we may see it reach a new level of sophistication in 2023. With increased processing power and better algorithms, machines could perform complex tasks that were previously thought impossible for computers to achieve. This has the potential to revolutionize everything from healthcare and transportation to finance and manufacturing.
